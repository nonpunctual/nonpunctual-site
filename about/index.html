His dad had all the late 70's tech: pirated HBO, a Telex machine, a HAM radio with a giant tower in the backyard & an ARPANET terminal with a phone coupler modem on which we used to play Oregon Trail. Email Champion ✉️

Sometimes people complain about the communication systems they are forced to use. Especially at work. Microsoft Teams (Microsoft “anything”, actually…) is a favorite punching bag.

Also a punching bag: email. Why?

The plain-text email system (along with algebra, calculus, computers, fire, flight, geometry, the lever, photography, regular expression syntax, the screw, UNIX, the wheel & writing) is one of the great human achievements. (There, I said it.)

I've had people say this (almost exactly) to me:

I wish I had some kind of system that was easy to search & kept all of my conversations in order forever. I would get so much done if I could just find my conversations.

Congrats bruh or ma'am. You just invented email.

Late Person ⏰

I am too late to be publishing my 1st website.

Me being a late person is infamously well-documented. Now, more so.

It’s the main theme of my life.

Am I proud of it? No.

Do I come by it honestly? Yes. My father was a very, very late person. I think, overall, maybe later than me.

Do I believe that it’s my worst trait or anyone’s worst trait? No. I forgive people who are not on time because, morally, I have no choice but to do so for the amount of time I’ve made people wait for things.

Do I believe that certain people unfairly succeed in the world because their main skill is punctuality? Yes & it’s ridiculous.

Time is an artificial construct. If you are always on time, I may hate you.
